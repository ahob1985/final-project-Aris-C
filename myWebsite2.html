The Hidden Genius Project trains and mentors black male youth in technology creation, entrepreneurship, and leadership skills to transform their lives and communities.
The Hiden Genius Project focuses their efforts on teaching black male youth useful
life skills to change the narrative written by others. The Hidden Genius Project taught me about HTML, CSS, and javascript, which helped me build this website.
